Project Name
. Electric Vehicles vs Petrol Cars Analysis
A Comparative Study of Cost, Efficiency, and Environmental Impact
________________________________________
Project Idea
The Future of Mobility in the world
The new challenge of smart urban mobility and Alternative Fuels 
Electric vehicles (EVs) are a promising technology for achieving a sustainable transport sector in the future, due to their very low to zero carbon emissions, low noise, high efficiency, and flexibility in grid operation and integration.
This dataset compares Electric Vehicles (EVs) and Petrol (Gasoline) Vehicles based on different features such as cost, performance, efficiency, and environmental impact.
