Project Name
Electric Vehicles vs Petrol Cars Analysis,
A Comparative Study of Cost, Efficiency, and Environmental Impact
________________________________________
Project Idea
This project focuses on analysing cost, performance, efficiency, and environmental impact of Electric Vehicles (EVs) and Petrol (Gasoline) Vehicles to understand the factors that contribute to change The Future of Mobility in the world achieving a sustainable transport sector in the future, due to their very low to zero carbon emissions, low noise, high efficiency, and flexibility in grid operation and integration this is The new challenge of smart urban mobility and Alternative Fuels.
